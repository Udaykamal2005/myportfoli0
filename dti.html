About Vermicomposting

Vermicomposting is the process of using worms to decompose organic food waste, turning it into a nutrient-rich material capable of supplying necessary nutrients to help sustain plant growth.

Benefits

  • Environmentally friendly
  • Improves soil structure
  • Reduces waste
